  • Journey Starts~

    I am Isaac Huang

  • 5th Aug 1986

    A Boy Was Born

    A 3.5kg baby was born in a town in Taipei on 5th Aug 1986. His name is Isaac Huang and his journey had just started.


  • 1st Jul 1998 - 30 Jun 2004

    He Was A Good Boy

    From primary school to high school, he was always the top student in the class.


  • 1st Jul 2004 - 30 Jun 2008

    Life in Uni

    As he loved figures and big data so much, he enrolled in Applied Mathematics in National Chung Hsing University in Taiwan.


  • 10th May 2009 - 9 May 2010

    Teacher in The City, Manila

    Every man in Taiwan has to serve in the army. However, Isaac chose to serve the country in a different way, and he was luckily chosen to present his country to teach Chinese and Mathematics in Manila in The Philippines.


  • 1st Jul 2011 - 30 Jun 2012

    Master's Degree in Data Science

    He then continuted his education in data science in National Taiwan University of Science and Technology.


  • 5th Aug 2012 - 4th Aug 2014

    Gap Years in Australia

    The research wasn't going well, so he deferred his degree and came to Australia, and he fell in love with his partner and the city, Perth.


  • 14th Oct 2014 - 24th Jul 2020

    Life in Perth

    He decided to stay in Perth with his partner. He then worked for another 6 years to rebuild his life in Perth.


  • 27th Jul 2020 - The Present

    Back to School

    It was one of his regrets that he didn't finish his master's degree, so he decided to enrol in Master of IT at UWA. He had learnt and grown so much ever since.


  • The Future

    To Be Continued

    The adventure is still to be continued and he's positive with the bright future in front of him and will come back to update this timeline~
